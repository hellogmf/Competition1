BASIC LEVEL
6- 18 Years






  • ~ Design anything related to Space.

  • ~Dimension: 9.5 X 9.5 cm

  • ~Paper type: 300gsm cardstock

Frequently Asked Questions
About XYZ

How many designs can I submit?

There is no limit to the number of projects being submitted for 3D models. But the posters should be only one per head.

Who can participate?

Participation is open to all the Givemefive students aged up to 18 years old. Poster making and Satellite 3D model is open to individuals. For Mars colonization 3D model it can be either individual participation or a group comprised of a minimum of 2 up to a maximum of 6 students.

When will the winners be announced?

The winners will be announced in April 2021.

If I have a problem with my entry, or a question about the competition, whom should I contact?

Please use the Givemefive email: hello@givmefive.ai

Does my entry need to be in English?

Yes.Your report must be completed in English.
